Before production release:

1. Confirm Firebase project is production-ready.
2. Confirm Firestore rules are deployed.
3. Confirm Storage rules are deployed.
4. Confirm Cloud Functions are deployed.
5. Confirm Firebase Authentication providers are enabled:
   - Email/password
   - Google, if Google sign-in is offered in the production build
6. Confirm authorized domains include the hosted admin web domain.
7. Confirm Android OAuth client uses package:
   com.rov_japheth.kabisigmobile
8. Add Android SHA-1/SHA-256 fingerprints from EAS credentials/Play signing to Firebase and Google Cloud OAuth.
9. Confirm iOS OAuth client uses Bundle ID:
   com.rov_japheth.kabisigmobile
10. Keep real secrets out of Git. Use EAS secrets or environment variables.
