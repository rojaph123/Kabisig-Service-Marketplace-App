Permissions used by Kabisig:

Location:
- Purpose: Optional GPS/autofill for booking address, customer profile location, and post location.
- User-facing reason: Kabisig uses your location only when you choose to autofill a booking address or save your profile location.

Photos/videos:
- Purpose: Service request media, chat media, proof of work, payment screenshots, worker documents, and portfolio/work samples.
- User-facing reason: Kabisig lets you choose photos or videos for service requests, chats, proof of work, payment screenshots, and worker verification documents.

Notifications:
- Purpose: Booking updates, chat alerts, worker approval updates, payment/commission notices, review prompts, and account alerts.
- User-facing reason: Kabisig sends booking, chat, payment, worker approval, and account update notifications.

Internet/network:
- Purpose: Account login, Firebase data, uploads, maps, notifications, and admin operations.

Sensitive permissions not intentionally used:
- Background location
- SMS
- Call log
- Contacts
- Microphone
- Camera
