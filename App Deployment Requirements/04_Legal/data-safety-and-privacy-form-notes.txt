Use these notes when completing Google Play Data safety and Apple App Privacy.

Data collected:
- Name
- Email address
- Phone number when provided
- Profile photo
- Customer address and optional GPS pin
- Worker identity/profile/application details
- Work samples and uploaded documents
- Booking details, schedules, notes, and attachments
- Messages and support requests
- Reviews, ratings, complaints, and reports
- Payment records, commission bills, payment screenshots, reference numbers, and payment dates
- Device/session data and push notification tokens

Primary purposes:
- App functionality
- Account management
- Booking coordination
- Worker verification
- Payment and commission review
- Fraud prevention and safety
- Customer support
- Notifications
- Legal/accounting/admin operations

Third-party services:
- Firebase Authentication
- Firestore
- Firebase Storage
- Firebase Cloud Functions
- Expo notifications
- Google sign-in
- Google Maps links/embed
- Hosting provider for admin web

Data sharing:
- Booking details are shared between customers, assigned workers, and admins as needed.
- Approved worker profile details are visible to customers.
- Admins can review records for operations, safety, payments, complaints, and support.
- Third-party infrastructure providers process data needed to operate the app.

Security:
- Authenticated access
- Role-based admin access
- Firebase rules
- Storage controls
- Audit records

Data deletion:
- Users can request deletion through Help or support@kabisig.app.
- Some records may be retained for legal, safety, accounting, active booking, payment, commission, dispute, or fraud-prevention reasons.
