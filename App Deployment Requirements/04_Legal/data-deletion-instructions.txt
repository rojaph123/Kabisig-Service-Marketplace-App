Users may request deletion by opening Kabisig > Profile > Help or emailing support@kabisig.app from the email address linked to their Kabisig account.

The request should include:
- Full name
- Account email
- Role: customer or worker
- Clear request to delete or deactivate account data

Kabisig may retain records needed for active bookings, payment records, commission billing, complaints, fraud prevention, accounting, safety, legal obligations, or audit history.
